About

Systems thinking.
Clear strategy.
Real outcomes.

Across my career I've led product, engineering, marketing, analytics, and people teams through very different phases — from hypergrowth and IPO preparation to a near-total revenue collapse during COVID and rebuilding from there.

Experiences like these reinforce a simple lesson: great companies are built on clear systems, not heroics. Whether through a focused workshop or longer advisory engagement, my goal is always the same — connect customer value, product decisions, and business outcomes through repeatable systems.
